Description

This model effectively demonstrates the potential harm that a worker may incur by not adhering to appropriate safety measures when using a grinder. Its purpose is to educate employees during training sessions on the correct usage of the equipment, as well as the safety precautions they should follow. By showcasing the consequences of improper usage, the model serves as a powerful tool for reinforcing the importance of safety protocols and promoting a culture of responsibility in the workplace. Through such training initiatives, employees gain the necessary knowledge and skills to operate machinery safely, reducing the risk of accidents and injuries.
